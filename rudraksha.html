Rudraksha

Rudraksha beads are the sacred seeds of the Rudraksha tree, deeply revered in Hindu mythology. According to ancient texts, when Lord Shiva opened his eyes after prolonged meditation to destroy the demon Tripura, tears fell from his eyes. Where those tears touched the earth, Rudraksha trees grew.

Lord Brahma granted these divine fruits the power to free mankind from sins and sorrows, providing both Bhukti (enjoyment) and Mukti (salvation). Wearing genuine Rudraksha beads is believed to bring peace, prosperity, and spiritual growth.
