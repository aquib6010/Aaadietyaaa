Raksha Kavach

Raksha Kavach (रक्षा कवच) is a sacred protective amulet rooted in ancient Vedic traditions. The word "Kavach" literally means "armor" — a divine shield that protects the wearer from negative energies, evil eyes, black magic, and life's obstacles.

Our Raksha Kavach collection is energized with powerful mantras by experienced Vedic scholars. Each Kavach is dedicated to a specific deity, channeling their divine protection and blessings to the wearer. These Kavach are especially beneficial when worn during Navratri, festivals, and during difficult planetary transits.

We offer 10 types of Raksha Kavach, each dedicated to a different deity and serving unique spiritual purposes — from the powerful Ram Tantra Kavach for whole family protection, to the nine forms of Maa Durga (Navdurga) for specific life challenges.
