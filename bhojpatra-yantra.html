Bhojpatra Yantra

Bhojpatra (भोजपत्र) is the sacred bark of the Himalayan Birch tree (Betula utilis), revered since Vedic times as one of the most auspicious materials for writing mantras, yantras, and sacred texts. Ancient rishis and sages used Bhojpatra to inscribe powerful mantras that channeled divine energies for protection, prosperity, and spiritual growth.

A Bhojpatra Yantra is a sacred geometric diagram inscribed on Bhojpatra using specific inks (often saffron or ashtagandha paste) during auspicious muhurtas. When energized with proper Vedic mantras, these yantras become powerful conduits of cosmic energy, providing the wearer with divine blessings and protection.

Our Navakshari Yantras are inscribed on Bhojpatra and mounted on precious metals — Gold and Silver. The Navakshari mantra is one of the most powerful Shakti mantras, invoking the blessings of Maa Durga for complete protection and wish fulfillment.
