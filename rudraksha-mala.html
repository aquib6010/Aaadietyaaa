📿 Rudraksha Mala

Rudraksha Mala is a sacred beaded necklace deeply revered in Hinduism and Buddhism for its spiritual and healing properties. Traditionally used for meditation, mantra chanting (Japa), and spiritual growth, each bead is believed to carry unique vibrational frequencies that connect the wearer with divine energy.

A traditional Rudraksha Mala consists of 108 beads plus one Guru bead. The number 108 holds deep cosmic significance — representing the wholeness of existence. Whether used for chanting "Om Namah Shivaye" or worn around the neck, the Mala acts as both a spiritual tool and a powerful protective amulet.
